# Pricing & Packaging Strategy: Enterprise AI Coding Governance

## Overview

The market has standardized on per-seat pricing at $19-40/user/month. We can justify premium pricing by governance and compliance features while maintaining our core competency as a strong model provider.

## Pricing Tiers

### Pro — $20/user/month
*Target: Individual developers / small teams / startups*

- Unlimited AI coding tool usage
- Personal usage dashboard
- Community support

---

### Team — $40/user/month
*Target: Growing teams (or lower middle-market companies) that need visibility*

Everything in Pro, plus:
- Opus model access + latest models
- Team analytics & reporting
- Data export (JSON)
- Upgraded-tier / higher availability for support

---

### Enterprise — $60/user/month + Platform Fee
*Target: Organizations that need governance and compliance (ex. regulated industries)*

Everything in Team, plus:
- Full governance dashboard with REST API
- Compliance reports
- Dedicated priority customer support + exec engagement

We can add a platform fee component for the enterprise with additional features and analytics.

---

### Enterprise Plus — Custom
*For the largest enterprises with custom or specialized requirements (ex. F100)*

Everything in Enterprise, plus:
- Dedicated/on-premise infrastructure
- Custom integrations & compliance frameworks
- White-glove service as needed (setup or custom configuration, for example)
- Custom SLA (up to 99.99%)

---

## Go-To-Market Approach

1. **Land-and-Expand**: Start with Team, grow into Enterprise
2. **Free Pilots**: 30-day trials
3. **Annual Incentives**: 30% discount for annual prepay
4. **Volume Discounts**: Based on seat count
5. **Startup Program**: High discount for accelerator companies
